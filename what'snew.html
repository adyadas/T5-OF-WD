Bihar Tourism Policy 2023:

In December 2023, the Bihar state cabinet approved the Bihar Tourism Policy 2023, aiming to attract substantial investments and boost tourist footfall. The policy offers financial incentives to investors, including a capital subsidy of 30% for investments up to ₹10 crore, 25% for investments up to ₹50 crore, and 25% for investments exceeding ₹50 crore, with a maximum limit of ₹25 crore. These incentives are structured to be disbursed in phases: 50% at the commencement of commercial operations, 25% after two years, and the remaining 25% after five years. Additional benefits include 100% reimbursement of land conversion fees, stamp duty, and registration fees, along with 80% reimbursement of GST for up to five years. Development of Five-Star Hotels:

To cater to the growing number of tourists, Bihar is expanding its hospitality sector with the construction of five new five-star hotels. Two of these hotels are being developed in Gaya, while three are underway in Patna.
